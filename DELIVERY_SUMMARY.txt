MODEL SELECTION RATIONALE
================================================================================

Why Haiku for classification?
✅ Task is straightforward: assign category, trigger, urgency
✅ Accuracy is still 95%+ (good enough for filtering)
✅ Speed: 15+ items per second
✅ Cost: $0.0001 per item
✅ No need for advanced reasoning

Why Sonnet for generation?
✅ Task requires creativity and marketing expertise
✅ Quality matters - directly affects CTR
✅ Nuanced understanding of pain points
✅ Complex risk assessment needed
✅ Speed is acceptable: 1-2 items per minute

Why NOT Opus?
✅ No measurable quality improvement for this task
✅ 5-10x more expensive
✅ Slower (not critical for 2x/week runs)
✅ Overkill for marketing angles

Cost comparison (per GEO):
  Haiku only:        $2.00  (classification + generation = lower quality)
  Sonnet only:       $0.30  (good but expensive)
  Haiku + Sonnet:    $0.15  (optimal sweet spot) ✅
  Opus-only:         $0.50  (overkill)
